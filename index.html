Roadmap
30 · 60 · 90 Day Plan
30
Days — Listen & Map
Understand the landscape before touching it
  • Shadow every team that touches the brand — product, partnerships, athlete relations, social
  • Audit current PR cadence, agency relationships, and media coverage quality
  • Identify 3–5 under-leveraged story angles sitting in existing data
  • Map executive visibility: where is Will Ahmed showing up vs. where he should be
  • Build trust with comms team — understand what's blocked, what's brewing
60
Days — Build & Prioritize
Establish infrastructure and a point of view
  • Deliver a communications strategy brief to leadership — owned, earned, and executive
  • Establish editorial calendar aligned to product roadmap and cultural moments
  • Rationalize agency roster; set clear KPIs and accountability structures
  • Begin media relationship development — top 15 priority journalists and editors
  • Propose framework for crisis communications and spokesperson protocols
90
Days — Activate & Measure
Launch something. Show what this function can do.
  • Execute first fully owned comms campaign from brief to coverage
  • Deliver monthly comms dashboard to leadership — share of voice, sentiment, tier-1 placements
  • Activate at least one earned media spike tied to a product or data story
  • Present 12-month communications roadmap with resourcing and investment ask
  • Assess team structure and surface any hiring or restructuring recommendations

Communications Strategy
📡
Earned media as brand truth
WHOOP's credibility lives in third-party validation. The strategy is to feed journalists with irresistible data stories — not press releases — and earn coverage that advertising can't buy.

target placement standard

Executive voice as a distribution channel
Will Ahmed is one of the most credible voices in performance tech. The communications function should systematize that — turning his insights into a programmatic content engine across media, podcasts, and keynotes.

Data as the unfair advantage
No fitness platform has WHOOP's longitudinal behavioral data at this scale. A research communications program — peer-reviewed, press-released, and athlete-validated — creates perpetual media gravity.
